Meme Voice Actor
Privacy Policy

Put your voice on a meme

Put your voice on a meme video or record your voice on your own random videos. The app has a few meme video's bundled but it can use any animated memes that are already on your phone.

Available on the Google Play Store

  

Multiple audio tracks

You can record multiple audio tracks with the phone's microphone and when you are done and satisfied with the result, the app will combine all audio tracks and create your new voice meme.

Sound effects

You can also add sound effects to your memes, check the in-app store to see which sound effects are available.

Reporting problems.
